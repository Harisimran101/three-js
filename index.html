Ask your Pharmacist, Doctor or nurse practitioner about this product.

Anapen® contains adrenaline (epinephrine) as its only active ingredient. Anapen® is not a substitute for subsequent emergency medical or haspital care. Call 000 immediately after administering Anapen®. An additional dose of Anapen® may be needed while waiting for emergency medical services. Anapen® can be obtained from a pharmacy with a prescription, or without a a prescription where a pharmacist is satisfied the customer has been assessed by an appropriate health practitioner as needing 4 ALLERGY to carry adrenaline and the health practitioner has provided the customer with a formal action plan for anaphylaxis.
